Who I Am

Engineer. Architect. Builder.

I started my career as a software engineer and spent two decades building things that scale — from distributed systems to large-scale cloud infrastructure. Over the last 8 years, I specialized in AWS cloud architecture and platform modernization, working with startups and enterprises across the UK and Europe from my base in Serbia.

I've helped companies reduce infrastructure costs by up to 40%, migrate complex workloads to cloud-native architectures, and move faster with automation-first principles using Terraform and modern CI/CD pipelines.

Today, I'm focused on something I believe is a generational shift. I'm Co-Founder of CarryYourAI, where we're building AI-powered smart glasses — making human-AI interaction wearable, ambient, and always with you. I bring the same engineering rigour that made production systems resilient and scalable to the challenge of building the next computing platform.
